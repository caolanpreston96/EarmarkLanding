Pension Tracker
Alternative to Moneyhub

By Caolan Preston, CF30 April 2026 5 min read

Moneyhub was one of the few UK apps that aggregated pension pots from multiple providers into a single view. While earmarkIQ doesn’t replicate pension pot aggregation directly, it takes a different approach — helping you optimise your pension contributions through a salary sacrifice calculator, AI tax planning, and personalised recommendations that can save you thousands over your career.

⚠️ Moneyhub Shutdown Notice

Moneyhub closes its consumer app on 14 August 2026. All pension pot aggregation, provider connections, and historical pension data will become unavailable. Consider using the government’s State Pension checker and individual provider portals alongside earmarkIQ.

What Moneyhub Offered

Moneyhub’s pension tracker was a standout feature. It connected to pension providers via Open Finance integrations and aggregated multiple pension pots into a single dashboard. You could see your total pension value, contributions, and growth across workplace pensions, SIPPs, and the State Pension.

For users with pensions scattered across multiple employers and providers, this was invaluable — finally seeing your total retirement picture in one place.

earmarkIQ’s Pension Approach

earmarkIQ takes a different but complementary approach to pensions. Rather than aggregating existing pension pots (which requires proprietary pension provider integrations), earmarkIQ focuses on optimising your pension contributions to maximise your retirement savings:

Salary sacrifice calculator — Our free salary sacrifice tool shows exactly how much you’d save in tax and National Insurance by increasing pension contributions through salary sacrifice. For many UK professionals, this is worth £500–£3,000+ per year in tax savings.
AI tax planning — Ask IQ analyses your income and spending to recommend optimal pension contribution levels, considering your tax band, student loan repayments, and childcare benefit thresholds.
Contribution tracking — earmarkIQ tracks pension contributions that appear in your bank statements via Open Banking, showing you what’s going into your pension each month.

📝 Honest Note

earmarkIQ does not aggregate pension pot balances from multiple providers like Moneyhub did. If you specifically need to see all your pension pot values in one place, you may also want to use the government’s pension tracing service or your individual provider portals. earmarkIQ’s value is in optimising what you contribute, not viewing what you’ve accumulated.

Migration Guide: 3 Simple Steps

Note your pension pot values from Moneyhub

Before 14 August 2026, screenshot or export your pension pot values and provider details from Moneyhub. Save your provider names, pot values, and contribution amounts for reference.

Use the salary sacrifice calculator

Visit earmarkIQ’s salary sacrifice calculator to see how much you could save by optimising your pension contributions. Enter your salary, current contribution rate, and see the tax savings instantly.

Connect your bank and ask IQ about pensions

Sign up for earmarkIQ and connect your bank accounts. Ask IQ can then analyse your income, tax position, and spending to recommend the optimal pension contribution level for your specific situation.
