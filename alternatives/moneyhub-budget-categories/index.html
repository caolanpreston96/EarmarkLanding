⚠️ Moneyhub Shutdown Notice

Moneyhub closes its consumer app on 14 August 2026. All budget categories, spending limits, and historical spending data will become unavailable. Export your data and migrate before the deadline.

What Moneyhub Offered

Moneyhub categorised your transactions into spending groups like groceries, transport, entertainment, and bills. You could set budget limits for each category and track your progress through the month. It was a solid, manual budgeting system — but it required you to set everything up yourself and often mis-categorised transactions.

earmarkIQ’s AI Budget Categories

earmarkIQ takes a fundamentally different approach. Instead of just categorising what you’ve already spent, it starts by allocating what you should spend when your salary arrives. The AI analyses your transaction history and automatically creates a spending plan:

12 AI-powered categories — Transactions are automatically sorted into Housing, Bills, Subscriptions, Groceries, Eating Out, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Health, Education, Finances, and General.
Salary allocation — On payday, earmarkIQ splits your income: Bills (10%), Savings (1%), Invest (0%), Discretionary (89%) — personalised to your actual spending patterns.
Free-to-spend figure — After all commitments, you see exactly how much guilt-free money you have left (£2.6k on a £2,942 salary).

✦ AI Financial Profile

earmarkIQ goes beyond categories to build your Money Personality. It analyses spending rhythms, payday spikes (+21% average), and patterns to identify your type — like “The Feast or Famine Spender” — and provides personalised recommendations to improve your financial health.
