Where Monzo Genuinely Wins

It’s a bank. Monzo gives you a real bank account, debit card, and instant spending notifications the moment you tap your card. earmarkIQ is not a bank — it’s a financial management layer. You still need a bank account somewhere.

Available today with millions of users. Monzo has been live for years and has millions of UK customers. earmarkIQ launches mid-2026. If you need something right now, Monzo is an excellent choice.

Salary Sorter. Monzo’s Salary Sorter is a basic but working payday split — you set rules to move money into Pots when your salary lands. It’s not AI-powered and only works within Monzo, but it works today.

Pots are simple and effective. Monzo’s Pots let you ring-fence money for specific purposes (rent, holiday, emergency fund). They’re visual, satisfying, and easy to understand.

Joint account for couples. Monzo offers joint accounts where both partners get a card. The limitation is both partners must have Monzo, but the feature works well within that constraint.

Real-time transaction notifications. As a bank, Monzo can push instant notifications the moment you spend — before you even put your card away. No budgeting app can match this speed because they rely on Open Banking data which has a slight delay.

Where earmarkIQ Wins

Cross-bank visibility. This is the fundamental difference. earmarkIQ sees ALL your accounts — Monzo, Barclays, HSBC, Lloyds, Nationwide, Marcus, and 50+ more — in one place. Monzo only sees Monzo. If you have money spread across multiple banks (and most people do), earmarkIQ gives you the complete picture.

AI salary allocation is smarter. earmarkIQ’s AI analyses your spending patterns across all accounts and recommends optimal splits. Monzo’s Salary Sorter is manual rules within one bank. earmarkIQ’s allocation adapts to your behaviour and works across all your accounts via Payment Initiation.

Subscription price creep detection. earmarkIQ alerts you the moment any subscription increases its price, showing old price, new price, and annual impact. Monzo shows recurring payments but won’t flag price increases — you’d only notice when the amount changes. We explain how this compounds in our UK subscription tracking guide.

Ask IQ AI advisor. earmarkIQ’s AI advisor has full context of your transactions across ALL banks, bills, and goals. Ask “Can I afford a holiday?” and get a data-backed answer that considers your Monzo balance, your Barclays savings, and your HSBC credit card. Monzo has no AI advisor.

Partner finance without switching banks. earmarkIQ’s partner mode works regardless of which banks you and your partner use. Monzo’s joint account requires both partners to be Monzo customers. See our guide to managing money as a couple.

Pro financial tools. earmarkIQ includes 10 planning tools — debt payoff planner, tax optimiser, salary raise calculator, what-if simulator, investment calculator, and more — that Monzo doesn’t offer.

No need to switch your bank account. earmarkIQ works with your existing banks. You don’t need to move your salary, switch direct debits, or open a new account. Monzo requires you to switch (or at least use it as your main account) to get full value from its budgeting features.
